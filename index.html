Welcome!

We are the Business Professionals of America Chapter in Marshall. Since 2008, we have welcomed new generations of students planning to enter various work fields. Join us to excel and achieve your goals!

In the 2022-2023 year, our BPA students achieved a record-breaking seven first-place recognitions! This is only the beginning. Join us in making history and preparing for the future.

BPA is an organization for every student. We are known for our friendly, cooperative vibe and supportive environment. Join us to build your skills and thrive in the workforce.
