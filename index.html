Welcome!

We are the Business Professionals of America Chapter in Marshall. We started in 2008 and since then welcomed new generations of students planning on entering into various work fields.

















We are known for providing great encouragement and support to all members. Our advisors, Ms. Kellen and Ms. Timmerman, are dedicated to bettering each and every member to reach their goals. In the year 2022 to 2023, BPA students set a new record in achieving seven first-place recognitions. However, that is only the beginning. Join us in continuing to break our records and preparing for the future.






We are an organization for every student! We are NOT a super competitive activity that will constrain your time. We are known for having a very friendly, cooperative vibe and a supportive environment.

BPA is an organization dedicated towards bettering and preparing students to enter into the work force. It is a community of people inspired to succeed and to help each other to succeed.
